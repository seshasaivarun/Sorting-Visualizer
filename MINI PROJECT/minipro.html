SORTING TECHNIQUES

SORTING:-

Sorting is any process of arranging items systematically. It can be divided as follows:-
(1)Ordering: arranging items in a sequence ordered by some criterion.
(2)Categorizing: grouping items with similar properties.

Types of Sorting Techniques:-

(1)Bubble Sort.
(2)Selection Sort.
(3)Merge Sort.
(4)Insertion Sort.
(5)Quick Sort.
(6)Heap Sort.
